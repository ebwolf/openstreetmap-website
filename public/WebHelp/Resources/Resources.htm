Resources

Acceptable ways to locate information on a structure feature include:

 

  • Website of the structure feature (e.g., a school website, a city's website of libraries, etc.)

 

  • Phone call to confirm the name and address of the structure feature

 

  • Local knowledge (e.g., you already know the name of the elementary school or library in your neighborhood)

 

  • Visual confirmation (e.g., in person,or using online maps with "on-the-ground" views of the street)

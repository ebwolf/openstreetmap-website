View Tab

The "View" tab is the default tab you see when you log into the Potlatch online editor.

 

The "View" tab is used for moving around the map, zooming in and out, searching, and accessing important links:

 

Note:  You cannot edit structure features in the "View" tab.  You must select the "Edit" tab to edit structure features.

 

The background picture, or base map, displayed in the "View" tab can be changed.  The default background is the USGS Topographic Map.  Other resources include NAIP Imagery (aerial photographaphy) and The National Map.

Instructions for Creating a Potlatch Account

 

Your Potlatch account will be used to access the online map editor we use to edit and collect structure features.

 

 

Follow these steps to create a Potlatch account:

 

STEP 1:     Go to http://navigator.er.usgs.gov/.

 

STEP 2:     Click "sign up" in the top right corner.

 

STEP 3:     Create a Potlatch user account.  

 

Please use your full e-mail address as your user name.

 

Note:  please ignore the alternate login sections

 

STEP 4:     You will receive an e-mail asking you to confirm your Potlatch account.

 

Click the link in the e-mail message.

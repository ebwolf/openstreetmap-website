Step 6: Check the "Finished Editing" Box

 

Since I finished adding the new feature, I click inside the box labeled “Finished Editing”.  

 

This box is located under the "Name" field in the "Required" tab:

 

 

 

Clicking inside this box changes the outline of the feature to green and allows me to see what structure features I have edited.
